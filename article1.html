Project 1

Intel-IrriS: Smart Low-Cost Irrigation for Smallholders

Type: IoT
Publication Date: June 2025

Intel-IrriS addresses the challenges smallholders face in adopting smart farming technologies by proposing a cost-effective and locally manageable irrigation control system. Unlike conventional setups that rely on expensive cloud services, Intel-IrriS utilizes a decentralized, on-site architecture to reduce cost and complexity.

The system employs off-the-shelf sensors and Raspberry Pi-based gateways to create a fully autonomous solution. Leveraging LoRa communication, Intel-IrriS ensures long-range, low-power data transmission—ideal for rural deployments with limited connectivity. Edge intelligence is enabled using AI accelerators like Google Coral TPU, supporting real-time decision-making without cloud dependency.

An adaptive Decision Support System (DSS) integrates sensor feedback and deep learning forecasts to deliver precise irrigation recommendations. These forecasts include soil moisture predictions based on environmental parameters, enhancing water efficiency and crop health. The framework also supports energy-aware data transmission and selective model updates to optimize local resources.

The project adopts a co-design methodology involving agronomists, local farmers, and ICT experts. A Stakeholders & Experts Advisory Committee (SEAC) and a 30-month field piloting program guide system refinement through iterative feedback. Emphasis is placed on usability, resilience, and socio-economic relevance.

As a core team member, I actively contributed to the deployment of Intel-IrriS kits in the Oran region, performed on-site hardware assembly and testing, and co-led the development of deep learning models for soil parameter forecasting. Additionally, I participated in enhancing the system’s intelligence by integrating data-driven predictions into the DSS and optimizing sensor-gateway interactions for energy efficiency.

In summary, Intel-IrriS stands as a scalable, intelligent, and accessible solution for sustainable irrigation. Its fusion of IoT, embedded AI, and localized deployment strategies empowers smallholders with tailored technology, promoting agricultural resilience in resource-constrained settings.
